WEB DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

As a Full-Stack Developer, I strive to be as creatively skilled at design as I am proficient with programming. I have a strong desire to help startups expand and scale by creating applications that convey their brand aesthetic, incite customer engagement, and run on efficient code that is adaptable to the quickly changing times. Prior to learning software engineering, I worked with startups in industries ranging from FinTech to tourism and mobile marketing, and focused largely on customer acquisition and business development. This commercial background allows me to better understand business needs and potential gaps between commercially viable and technically realizable.

In my free time, I volunteer those skills to various causes, and use my art to take a stance on social and environmental issues. More than anything, I would like to continue collaborating with like-minded people to build products that have the power to improve lives, educate people, and advocate for important causes.
